About Us

We are the Tau Beta Chapter of Theta Tau, a professional national engineering fraternity for university students founded on on October 15, 1904 at the University of Minnesota in Minneapolis, Minnesota. We take pride in uniting all our members in pursuit of three primary goals: Professional Development, Community Service, and Brotherhood.

Our Purpose

The purpose of Theta Tau is to develop and maintain a high standard of professional interest among its members and to unite them in a strong bond of fraternal fellowship.
