About Us Tau Beta Chapter

We are Southern Methodist University's Tau Beta Chapter of Theta Tau, the foremost professional engineering fraternity founded on October 15, 1904 at the University of Minnesota in Minneapolis.

We unite our brothers around three pillars: Professional Development, Community Service, and Brotherhood.

The purpose of Theta Tau is to develop and maintain a high standard of professional interest among its members and to unite them in a strong bond of fraternal fellowship.
